Our Merch Vendors
We're excited to announce that we've partnered with our first merchandise vendor, Marauder Merchandise!

Based out of Kansas City and a Resonite user themselves, Marauder Merchandise has created for us two shirt designs! These products are screen printed by hand to ensure print longevity and quality.

By purchasing one of these shirts you can support not only the platform but one of it's creators!
